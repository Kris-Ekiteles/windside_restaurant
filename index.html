Our Story

Founded in 2020, Checkpoint has been serving exceptional cuisine in a warm and inviting atmosphere.

Our chef, with over 15 years of international experience, creates dishes that blend traditional techniques with modern flair. We source our ingredients locally whenever possible, supporting sustainable farming practices.

Every dish tells a story, and we invite you to be part of ours. Whether it's a romantic dinner, family celebration, or business meeting, our team is dedicated to making your experience memorable.
